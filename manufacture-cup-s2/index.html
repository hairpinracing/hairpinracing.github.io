02

How It Works

01
One Manufacturer, All Season
Every driver picks a single manufacturer at Round 1 and races cars from that brand exclusively for all 10 rounds. Multiple drivers can (and did) pick the same brand.
02
Gr.3 and Gr.4, Track to Track
The class alternates by round — some rounds run Gr.3, others Gr.4 — so your chosen manufacturer needs a competitive car in both categories.
03
Drop Your Worst Round
Each driver's single worst-scoring round (including a DNS/DNF) is dropped from their season total — but only finalized after the last race, so it's still an open question for anyone who hasn't had a bad round yet.
