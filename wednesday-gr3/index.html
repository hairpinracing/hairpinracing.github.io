The Season

Wednesday Night Gr.3 BOP · Season 1 · Complete

Nine weeks. Nine cars. Nine circuits.
One champion. One point.

From late January through April 2026, the Wednesday Night Gr.3 BOP Series ran a 9-week championship across American, European, and Asian circuits — one Gr.3 car per track, no repeats. Every driver on the same footing: BOP on, tuning locked to brake balance only. Barry took the title by a single point over RawRacingVR — and the battle for 3rd was just as close, with Weaksauce412 edging TVDV by one point as well.

How It Works

01
Pure BOP Racing
All drivers in the same Gr.3 class with Balance of Performance active. Tuning is locked to brake balance only — no setup advantage, just pace and racecraft.
02
Nine Cars, Nine Tracks
Three regions — Americas, Europe, Asia — three cars each. One car per track, no repeats across the season. Every race is a fresh machine on a fresh circuit.
03
Full Race Format
Grid start with fastest-first grid order. Mandatory tire compound change. Fuel consumption & tire wear at 6×. Refuel rate: 10L/sec. Randomized time of day and weather cycles. Light damage, no ghosting. 15 min practice beforehand.
