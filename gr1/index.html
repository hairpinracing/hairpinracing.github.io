Season 1

● Season 1 — 5 of 10 Rounds Complete · Through July 13, 2026

Prototypes under the lights.
Speedysully leads after Monza, with three rivals within 15 points.

Hairpin Racing's newest series puts everyone in LMDh and Group 1 prototypes for a straight individual championship — no teams, just 15 drivers, BOP on, and a car locked in by the field: hypercars for most rounds, Group C-era prototypes for the two "Vintage" rounds. Speedysully (91 pts) leads CtillVR (88), rollinsmoke (77), and Ncardw11 (73) with five rounds to go. Admin: Pawl.

⚠ Round 1 (Fuji, June 15) results were never posted to the league in full — the points already count toward the standings below, but the official finishing order was lost. The R1 column shown is reconstructed from point-total math (see the note under Championship Standings), not an official results post.

02

How It Works

01
Single Class, Individual
No teams — 15 drivers racing straight for the individual championship, one class, one grid, every round.
02
Two Car Tiers
Most rounds: pick from 4 mandatory-ownership LMDh hypercars, max two starts per car all season. Two "Vintage" rounds swap in Group C-era prototypes instead.
03
Pole + Fastest Lap Bonus
Standard finishing-position points, plus a bonus point each for qualifying pole and the race's fastest lap — stackable, so a dominant driver can out-score their finishing position alone.
